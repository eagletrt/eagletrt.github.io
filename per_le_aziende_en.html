Our partners

Partners

The support of sponsors plays a key role in the success and growth of our project, enabling us to turn our ideas into concrete realities. Mutual trust, unwavering commitment and shared passion are the pillars that sustain us, guiding us toward ever more ambitious goals.

Together, we are making a tangible contribution to shaping the future of innovation and sustainability in the context of Formula Student. The dedication and tenacity shown since the beginning has allowed our team to be in constant growth; this makes us always open to new collaborations with business and technical partners interested in supporting our initiative.

Every contribution we receive is a source of great gratitude for us and we ensure that each partner is properly recognized for its support. Our team also has strong ties with the territory, this value has allowed us to gain recognition from, among many others, institutions and industries in Trentino that are examples of how investment in our project translates into opportunities for more growth and visibility.
