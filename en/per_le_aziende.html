Why they sponsor us?

Although the team has only recently been formed, the feedback obtained from the institutional and industrial reality in Trentino has been surprising. Indeed, the team, especially thanks to the advantages that investing in a university project like this can bring, has earned the trust of many already established companies in the automotive field.
‍
The constant and profitable synergies created between the University of Trento and the productive fabric of Trentino are able to bring benefits on both short- and long-term horizons, allowing brands active in academic life to stand out from their competitors, affirming Trentino's excellence in technological development and innovation.
